## 👨‍🔬 About Me
I am a PhD student at **Warsaw University of Technology (WUT)** and an AI Safety researcher at **NASK - National Research Institute**.

My research explores the internal latent representations of generative models to better understand and interpret their behaviour. While my interests are broad, my primary focus is AI Safety, where I leverage these interpretability insights to develop efficient techniques for building more secure and reliable AI systems.
